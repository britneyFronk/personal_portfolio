A little bit about myself...

My name is Britney (Brit) Fronk, and I am from Boise, ID. That is pronounced {boy-C!}, and please don't automatically assume that I am from Meridian or Eagle or any other city that is not Boise. I may come across as an introverted gal, but when you spend enough time with me, I will eventually quote more movies than you have probably cared to watch. What can I say? I'm not like other girls. I am the oldest in my family of eight, and I can't imagine life without them and my several aunts, uncles, cousins and grandparents that came with the package deal. I am studying Public Relations at Brigham Young University, and I hope to one day use my education to assist Kanye West's presidential campaign when he tries to take the throne in 2020 (I hope it's not just a rumor).

I can always work a dress, pair of heels, and flashy earrings on occasion. However, I am always equally eager to come home, put on my sweat pants and cuddle up on the couch with my roommates to a good Hallmark Christmas movie at all times of the year. If there's one thing I want people to remember about me it is that I can make a mean drop biscuit. If you don't believe me, you will just have to try one yourself.
